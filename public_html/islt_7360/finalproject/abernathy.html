Robert Abernathy

Robert Abernathy was born in 1924. He was a science fiction author for a period between the 1940s and 1950s. He has not had a lot written about him or his life. He died in 1990. Information found for Robert Abernathy was found through a variety of resources. However, there are no biographical entries written about him in a reliable resource.
