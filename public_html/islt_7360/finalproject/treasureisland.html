Treasure Island

Back to top

Written by: Robert Louis Stevenson
Publication date: November 14, 1883

Summary

Back to top

Treasure Island is an adventure novel. Jim Hawkins works in an inn until a stranger comes in and dies. After his death, Jim finds a treasure map. Jim and a group decide to set an expedition to discover this treasure. Along the way, they encounter pirates and peril. Do they discover the treasure, or will they lose everything in this grand trip?
