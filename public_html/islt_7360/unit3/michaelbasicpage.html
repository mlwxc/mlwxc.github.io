Michael Lewis' Basic Webpage

This webpage is to express the ideas of the type of webpages I would like to make. On this page, I will list two different ideas for websites along with the items I would like to include.

First Idea

This webpage will be the starting point of my project. I am creating an index of books of all types. I want to make this a smart search, kind of like Google, but limited to just this website. I don't want to people to feel they have to leave this website in order to find what they are looking for. I would also like to make a favorite books log in. That way if they find something they would enjoy reading they would be able to instantly go back to the book and have all the relevant information they need. However, I do not want to make suggestions to the reader, because I feel this would limit their choices.

Items to Include

  1. Author
  2. Genre
  3. Popularity
  4. ISBN
  5. Reviews
  6. Smart search limited to the website.
  7. Ability to create a favorite list exclusive to a person.
There is one website that is similar to what I would like to make and that is goodreads.com. They allow people to create an account to favorite books and allow the site to make recommendations. People are able to comment and rate the books they have read. It also contains information on the authors and the list of books they have written. This is one the closest example of a website that I would like to make, but this one makes suggestions along with the rating system. I think this would cloud people's own judgements.

They wouldn't have to go through a subreddit to find the correct account.
